● Risk profile

Identify and prioritise the client's significant health and safety risks. This profile should reflect the organisation's most important operational, health, safety, environmental and business risks.

● Management system

Assess the client's health and safety management arrangements against the HSG65 plan, do, check, act cycle. For each duty: is it in place, is it adequate, and what action is needed where it falls short. The duty headings are seeded; add the requirement lines and references yourself.

● Review & feedback

A quality and progress picture for this client, with your rating tracked over time. Generate a feedback report to send.

● Monitoring

Monthly capture, quarterly review
